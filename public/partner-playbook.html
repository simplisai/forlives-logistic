Frequently asked questions

What is the “30-day cookie”? +

When someone clicks your link, their browser remembers your ID for 30 days. If they buy within that window — even days later — the sale is credited to you.

Do I need to install anything or touch code? +

No. Tracking is already built into the stores. You just join a program, copy your link and share it.

Can I promote more than one store? +

Yes. Each store is a separate program with its own link and code. Join them all — every sale is credited to the right store.

What about sales in Brazil (megavoice.store/br)? +

It's the same store with the /br path. Just use your code: https://megavoice.store/br?via=YOUR_CODE. Tracking works the same.

What do pending, payable and paid mean? +

pending: the commission is in a review/holding period. payable: released and ready for payout. paid: already paid to you.

Does the customer see that it's my referral? +

Yes — the Stripe checkout shows “Partner: Your Name”, transparently confirming the referral is yours.
